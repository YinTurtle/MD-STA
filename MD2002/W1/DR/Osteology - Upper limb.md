### Clavicle
![[Pasted image 20260210151045.png]]
#### Costoclavicular and Coracoclavicular ligament
Conoid ligament and trapezoid ligament are parts of the coracoclavicular ligament. Forces from the upper limb are transmitted by trapezius to the cervical spine and by the clavicle to the axial skeleton by the coracoclavicular and costoclavicular ligaments.
#### Sternoclavicular joint
Is the only point at which the superior appendicular skeleton articulates with the axial skeleton.
