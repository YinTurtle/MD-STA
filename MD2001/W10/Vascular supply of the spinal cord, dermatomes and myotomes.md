# ğŸ«€ **3. VENOUS DRAINAGE OF THE SPINAL CORD**

_(Slides 7â€“9)_

## **3.1 Longitudinal Spinal Veins**

- Run lengthwise on surface of cord
    
- **NO valves** â†’ blood flow can reverse  
    

## **3.2 Internal Vertebral Venous Plexus (Epidural Space)**

- Located **within epidural fat**
    
- Drains spinal cord  
    

## **3.3 External Vertebral Venous Plexus**

- Surrounds vertebrae externally
    
- Connects to **segmental veins â†’ azygos & lumbar veins**  
    

### **Clinical Importance (Slide 7):**

- Valveless plexus = route for **metastasis** (breast, prostate) to vertebral bodies.
    
- Explains why vertebral metastases are common.
    

The diagram on page 8/9 clearly shows the internal & external plexuses surrounding vertebrae.

### **6.2 Myotome + Motor Nerve (Slide 21)**

- Cell bodies in **ventral horn**
    
- Axons follow somite into limb bud / trunk muscle
