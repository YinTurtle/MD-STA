# **21. Study Designs (Slides 38â€“46)**

### **Cross-sectional**

- Snapshot in time
    
- Measures **prevalence**
    
- Identifies associations  
    

### **Case-control**

- Cases with outcome vs controls without
    
- Looks backward for exposures
    
- For rare diseases
    
- Produces **odds ratios**  
    

### **Cohort**

- Exposure â†’ outcome over time
    
- Prospective or retrospective
    
- Gives **risk**, **incidence**, **RR**  
    

### **Randomised Controlled Trials (RCTs)**

- Random allocation to exposure
    
- Gold standard for **intervention**
    
- Produces high-quality evidence

# **24. Bias (Slide 52)**

Bias = **systematic error** in:

- Data collected
    
- How collected
    
- How analysed
    
- How interpreted
    
- How reported
    

Effects:

- Incorrect conclusions about **causation** or **effectiveness**
