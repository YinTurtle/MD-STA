# **2. Why History Taking Matters (Slide 3)**

A medical history is the **first step in clinical reasoning**. It helps you:

- Form a **differential diagnosis**
    
- Place health in **clinical + social context**
    
- Identify **risk factors**
    
- Direct **examination**
    
- Direct **investigation & management**  
    

**Big picture:**  
History â†’ guides exam â†’ guides investigations â†’ guides management

> **80% of diagnoses in general practice are made from HISTORY alone.**
