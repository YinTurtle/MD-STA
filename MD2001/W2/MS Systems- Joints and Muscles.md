## 4. Synovial Joints (Diarthroses)

**Key Features:**

- **Articular cartilage:** Smooth, avascular hyaline surface â†’ frictionless.
    
- **Synovial membrane:** Secretes synovial fluid.
    
- **Synovial fluid:** Lubricates, nourishes cartilage.
    
- **Fibrous capsule:** Encloses joint.
    
- **Ligaments:** Reinforce and stabilize.
    
- **Bursae:** Fluid sacs to reduce friction.
    
- **Tendon sheaths:** Surround tendons (e.g. hand/foot).

## 7. Joint Diseases

### Osteoarthritis (OA)

- Degenerative, affects weight-bearing joints.
    
- Loss of articular cartilage, osteophyte formation.
    
- Pain, stiffness, deformity.
    

### Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA)

- Autoimmune inflammation of synovium.
    
- Destroys cartilage; joint deformity.
    
- Affects small joints; more common in women.
